# Effective Tweet and Post Structures

This document contains proven social media post templates and structures that can be used to create engaging content across platforms like Twitter, LinkedIn, and other social media channels.

## Post Structure Templates

### 2x3 Comparison
**Instructions:** Create a visual pattern interrupt. Break apart information for easy consumption.

**Example:**
```
Broke:     Working a 9-5
Wealthy:   Running a business  
Rich:      Investing in assets

Employee:  Trading time for money
Business:  Building systems that make money
Investor:  Making money work for you
```

### 80/20 Rule
**Instructions:** Present a principle based on the Pareto principle. Offer a concise tip for implementation.

**Example:**
```
80% of your day should be filled with doing things you enjoy, minimum.

80% listening
20% talking

Avoid unnecessary chatter that may leave you with regret

Think deeply, then respond
```

### Ability-Outcome Link
**Format:** "Your ability to _ determines your _ level."

**Example:** "Your ability to focus determines your success level."

4. Share industry insights

- Your content goals (educate, inspire, entertain)

- The platform you're posting on
