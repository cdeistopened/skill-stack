# Gold Markers Guide - Transcript Mining

Quick reference for phrases and patterns to search for when mining transcripts.

- "I had no idea what I was doing"
- "I completely bombed"
- "I was in over my head"

### Fear Language
- "I was terrified"
- "I was afraid"
- "I panicked"
- "I thought I was going to lose everything"

### Doubt Language

- "I didn't think I could"
- "I was ready to quit"
- "I thought it was impossible"

## Search Strategy

### Step 1: Automated Search
Use Find function (Cmd+F / Ctrl+F) to search for high-value phrases above.

### Step 2: Manual Scan
Read through for:
- Emotional intensity shifts
- Voice tone changes
- Moments where energy spikes
- Places where you felt surprised

### Step 3: Mark Timestamps
Tag each promising moment with timestamp for easy retrieval.

### Step 4: Context Check
For each marked moment, read 20 seconds before and after to ensure it works as a clip.
