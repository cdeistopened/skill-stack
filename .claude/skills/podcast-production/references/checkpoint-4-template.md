## CHECKPOINT 4 QUALITY GATES

**YouTube Show Notes File**:
- Title is clear and compelling
- Thumbnail specs are concrete (3-element visual)
- Cold open clips have exact timestamps
- Cliffhanger marked (STOP before resolution)
- YouTube description has hook + guest credibility
- Chapter timestamps match actual content

**Polished Transcript**:
- Readable formatting with clear speaker labels
- All quotes are verbatim
- Supports blog post integration
- No typos or unclear passages

**Blog Post**:
- ~1,000 words
- Clear narrative arc (Hook/Connection/Insight/Application/Relevance)
- Conversational voice (not formal)
- 3-5 verbatim quotes
- Guest bio with links
- SEO headers with target keywords

**Overall**:
- Both files are publication-ready
- Video team can execute from show notes file
- Blog can publish as-is
- All deliverables ready for handoff
