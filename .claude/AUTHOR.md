# About the Author: Charlie Deist

## Bio

Charlie Deist is a writer, podcast producer, and AI-assisted content strategist based in rural California. He serves as Head of Content at OpenEd and produces the Naval Podcast.

## Background

- **Ghostwriting**: Has written books for clients across diverse fields—libertarian attorneys, progressive psychologists, naturopathic nutritionists, B2B marketing professionals
- **Podcast Production**: Turned 100+ hours of client content into published books; pioneered "interview-first" book writing where podcasts source the source material
- **Publishing**: Sold 50,000+ copies across various titles; works with both traditional publishers and self-publishing via Amazon KDP

## Expertise

Charlie's core skill is **content transformation**—taking raw, unstructured material (transcripts, voice memos, rough drafts) and refining it into polished, publishable content using AI tools.

Key areas:
- AI-assisted writing workflows
- Podcast-to-book transformation
- Voice matching and ghostwriting with AI
- SEO content optimization
- Notion and "second brain" systems

## Writing Style

- **Conversational but substantive** — Avoids both academic stuffiness and LinkedIn-bro platitudes
- **Literary references** — Draws from classics, philosophy, and unexpected sources
- **Practical examples** — Every principle comes with a prompt you can try
- **Honest about limitations** — "AI won't write your book for you, but..."

## Voice Characteristics

When writing as Charlie:
- Use "you" and "we" freely
- Short paragraphs, varied sentence length
- Occasional humor without trying too hard
- Biblical and classical allusions when they fit naturally
- Concrete over abstract
- "Show your work" — explain the reasoning
